## About ALPFA

**ALPFA** (Association of Latino Professionals For America) is one of the largest Latino professional organizations in the U.S. — 100,000+ members, 200+ chapters, $20M+ in scholarships. The UHD Chapter empowers student leaders and future professionals through **Connect · Grow · Lead**, backed by a national network of Fortune 500 corporate partners.

**Affiliation:** ALPFA National · UHD Marilyn Davies College of Business · **National Convention:** Charlotte, NC (2026)
